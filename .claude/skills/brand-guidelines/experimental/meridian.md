# Meridian

**Structured tension.** High-contrast black and white with sharp coral punctuation. For creative agencies and design platforms where the brand itself must signal visual sophistication.

**Voice:** Confident, opinionated, selective. "Less, but better."

**Best fit:** Creative tools, agencies, design platforms.

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Size | Leading |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Unbounded | 900 | 54px | 0.95 |
| H1 | Unbounded | 700 | 36px | 1.05 |
| H2 | Unbounded | 700 | 22px | 1.2 |
| H3 | Libre Franklin | 600 | 17px | 1.35 |
| Body | Libre Franklin | 400 | 15px | 1.65 |
| Caption | Libre Franklin | 400 | 12px | 1.5 |
| Label | Libre Franklin | 700 | 10px | 1.4 |
| Code | Roboto Mono | 400 | 13px | 1.6 |

**Rationale:** Unbounded at 900 weight is visually arresting and unlike anything in the standard toolkit. Libre Franklin is a workhorse with neutral clarity that lets Unbounded dominate. The typographic contrast between display and body is extreme by design.

## Signature Detail

Hard offset shadows with zero blur. Cards appear to physically lift off the page. Primary CTA buttons use coral red `#E84040` as the shadow color instead of black.
