# Solen

**Airy Scandinavian.** White space as a design element. Sage green and sand on pure white.

**Voice:** Gentle, clear, encouraging. "Simple enough that it gets out of your way."

**Best fit:** Consumer apps, wellness, productivity.

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Size | Leading |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Fraunces | 400 | 50px | 1.08 |
| H1 | Fraunces | 400 | 34px | 1.2 |
| H2 | Fraunces | 400 | 23px | 1.3 |
| H3 | Nunito | 600 | 17px | 1.4 |
| Body | Nunito | 400 | 16px | 1.75 |
| Caption | Nunito | 400 | 13px | 1.6 |
| Label | Nunito | 700 | 11px | 1.4 |
| Code | Inconsolata | 400 | 14px | 1.6 |

**Rationale:** Fraunces is a soft-serif optical font with warmth and personality without formality. Nunito's rounded terminals echo the friendly softness. The pairing avoids both the sterile (all-sans) and the overwrought (luxury serif).

## Signature Detail

Maximum border-radius on every element. Buttons are fully pill-shaped (`border-radius: 9999px`). Combined with feather-light shadows, every element feels softly cushioned.
