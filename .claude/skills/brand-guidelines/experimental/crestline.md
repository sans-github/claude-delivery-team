# Crestline

**Warm precision.** Swiss grid discipline softened by amber warmth. For products that mean business but don't feel cold.

**Voice:** Assured, grounded, unhurried. "We have done the thinking so you don't have to."

**Best fit:** B2B SaaS, consulting, financial services.

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Size | Leading |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Cormorant Garamond | 600 | 52px | 1.05 |
| H1 | Cormorant Garamond | 600 | 36px | 1.15 |
| H2 | Cormorant Garamond | 600 | 26px | 1.25 |
| H3 | DM Sans | 600 | 18px | 1.35 |
| Body | DM Sans | 400 | 16px | 1.65 |
| Caption | DM Sans | 400 | 13px | 1.5 |
| Label | DM Sans | 600 | 11px | 1.4 |
| Code | IBM Plex Mono | 400 | 13px | 1.6 |

**Rationale:** Cormorant Garamond brings editorial gravitas to headings. DM Sans provides clean legibility in functional roles. Headings feel authored, body feels efficient.

## Signature Detail

A 1px top border in `#C97D2E` on primary cards and hero sections only. Not a full border -- just the top edge, like a ruled line beneath a heading.
